Our Story

The Beginning

Born from a Passion for Craft

Arkedia was founded in 2018 in the heart of Cairo by designer Nadia Farouk, with a singular vision: to create fashion that honours both the wearer and the world. What began as a small atelier producing limited-run pieces for a discerning local clientele quickly grew into something far larger.

Nadia's background in textile engineering and her travels across Italy, Japan, and Morocco shaped Arkedia's aesthetic — one that finds beauty in restraint, that values the feel of a fabric as much as its appearance, and that insists on honesty in every seam.

Today Arkedia ships to over 50 countries, collaborates with artisan suppliers across three continents, and remains committed to the founding principle: clothing should be worthy of the women who wear it.

Our Values

Quality

Our Commitment

Fashion That Respects the Planet

We believe that beautiful clothing and environmental responsibility are not in conflict. Every decision we make — from fibre selection to delivery packaging — is guided by our commitment to a lighter footprint.

  • GOTS-certified organic cotton and linen sourced from responsible mills
  • 100% recycled and plastic-free packaging across all orders
  • Carbon-neutral shipping through verified offset programmes
  • Fair Wage Certified production partners across Egypt, Portugal, and Japan
  • Arkedia Repair — free mending service to extend the life of your pieces
  • 1% of annual revenue donated to textile workers' welfare organisations
