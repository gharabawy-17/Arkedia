Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping within Egypt takes 2–4 business days. International orders typically arrive within 7–14 business days. Express international shipping (3–5 days) is available at checkout. All orders over $150 qualify for free standard shipping.
What is your returns policy?
We offer free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with tags attached. To start a return, simply email us at hello@arkedia.com with your order number and we'll send you a prepaid return label.
How do I find my correct size?
Each product page features a detailed size guide with chest, waist, and hip measurements for every size (XS through XL). We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to our size chart rather than going by your usual clothing size, as cuts vary. If you're between sizes, we generally suggest sizing up for a relaxed fit or down for a closer fit. Contact us if you need personalised advice.
Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We do not store card details — payments are processed by our certified payment partners.
Can I exchange an item for a different size?
Absolutely. Size exchanges are free and handled within 30 days of delivery. Contact us with your order number and the size you need. Subject to availability, we'll dispatch the replacement as soon as we receive your return. If the replacement size is out of stock, we'll issue a full refund.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes — we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and chosen shipping method. Please note that customs duties and import taxes may apply depending on your country; these are the responsibility of the recipient. We ship with DHL Express and provide full tracking for all international orders.
